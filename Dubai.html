Dubai

Dubai is a popular tourist destination and a significant source of income for the city. In 2016, the city welcomed 14.9 million overnight guests.[1]Based on the number of foreign visitors, Dubai was the fourth most visited city in the world in 2018.

things to know about Dubai

Dubai history

History

The discovery of oil in 1966 kick-started the development of present Dubai, however Sheikh Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum (ruler from 1958 till 1990) realised one day Dubai would run out of oil and started building an economy that would outlast it

Places Information

Places

Dubai tourism Nothing compares to Dubai tourism! Dubai is a city that astounds us with its richness, style and is ranked as the "Most Beautiful City" in The Ara World.
Dubai Tradional food Al Harees: A traditional Arabic dish typically consumed during festive occasions like Ramadan and weddings. It consists of wheat, meat (commonly chicken or lamb), and a combination of spices, slow-cooked to a porridge-like consistency.
Dubai Landmarks Landmarks in Dubai include both historic and modern structures. Some of which areDubai Museum, Hatta Heritage Village, Burj Khalifa - the tallest tower, The Dubai Mall - the largest mall and Palm Jumeirah - the largest man-made island.
Abra ride Abra rides in Dubai are an excellent way to immerse yourself in the city's heritage, witness the bustling water traffic, and enjoy the picturesque views along the Dubai Creek. It's a must-try experience for tourists and locals alike
Dubai Ice Rink Visiting Dubai Ice Rink can be a fun and enjoyable experience for ice skating enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. However, it's advisable to check the official website or contact Dubai Ice Rink directly for the most up-to-date information on operating hours, prices, and any additional guidelines or restrictions
Dubai Safari Park The park is home to a wide variety of animal species from different parts of the world, including mammals, birds, reptiles, and amphibians. Visitors can encounter animals such as lions, tigers, giraffes, zebras, elephants, gorillas, crocodiles, snakes, birds of prey, and many more.
Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo is a popular attraction located in The Dubai Mall, one of the world's largest shopping and entertainment destinations in Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Desert Safari Dubai The Dubai desert is a vast expanse of sand dunes and arid landscapes that surrounds the city of Dubai in the United Arab Emirates.
